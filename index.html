About me

Caroline graduated from the University of Southern California in December 2023 with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Business Administration. She recently completed her Master of Science in Computer Science at USC in December 2024.

With a strong foundation in computer science and business administration, Caroline's academic journey has equipped her with a unique blend of technical and strategic skills. Her passion lies in software development, algorithms, blockchain, and web development. As a dedicated master's student, she constantly seeks to expand her knowledge and contribute to the ever-evolving field of computer science. Caroline is enthusiastic about leveraging her expertise to create innovative solutions and make a meaningful impact in the world of technology.

As of March 2025, Caroline has joined Meta as a Production Engineer on the Natural Disasters team, where she focuses on infrastructure resilience and reliability in the face of large-scale events. She is passionate about building scalable and impactful systems that help communities during critical times.

In her free time, you can find Caroline hiking, walking on the beach, painting, and enjoying the outdoors. These activities not only provide a balance to her tech-focused pursuits but also reflect her appreciation for the beauty and serenity found in nature.
